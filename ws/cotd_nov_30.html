Forgive, that was rude!
Trigger: Gate, Soul
[C]All of your characters get Power +1000, Soul +1

Persona 5 continues with a bunch more interesting cards (also best girl, Futaba is so cute). We've got a bunch of cards fitting of a navigator. A 3/2 support, while increasingly more common, is still notable in the game due to the unique types of abilities that tend to be printed on these cards, and Futaba doesn't disappoint. A global 1.5k is a nice first effect, and a post-damage check is a good recent ability. The last ability on the 3/2 Futaba is a Climax discard for an anti-damage. While similar to "Something Precious" Nao Tomori from Charlotte, Futaba has the advantage of working in any slot, so it isn't quite as easily countered by untargetable characters.

The other notable Futaba allows us to search our Stock. This allows us to get cards out of Stock without destroying all of our Stock. It's not a large assortment of cards to look through (explaining the low cost) but its still a card for a Stock, which is really good. Note that the effect can't be used with only 1 Stock, as the cost is paid before the effect. Also notably is that the card can search for Events, so it can also be slotted into other Persona decks outside of Persona 5 (such as Execution.)

The last card is a Morgana, which is a standard beater that gets bigger as your other characters attack. Not much to say here, these cards are generally good tech options, provided the space.
