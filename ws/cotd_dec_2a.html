Weiβ Schwarz - Card of the Day, December 2nd, 2016

Captain of the Phantom Thieves Ryuji/SKULL
Trigger: None
Trait(s): <<Phantom Thief>>, <<Sports>>
[C] All of your other <<Phantom Thief>> or <<Sports>> characters get Power +500.
[A] When your character's Direct Attack's damage is not cancelled, choose a face-down card in your opponent's Memory and put it to the Waiting Room. If you do, search your deck for a <<Phantom Thief>> or <<Sports>> character, show it to your opponent, put it into your hand, and shuffle your deck.

Established Trade Ryuji
Trigger: None
Trait(s): <<Phantom Thief>>, <<Sports>>
[A] [Put a card from your hand to the Waiting Room] When this card is placed from hand to the Stage, you may pay the cost. If you do, choose a 「Calling Card」 from your Waiting Room and return it to your hand.

Calling Card
Trigger: None
If your opponent's Memory has no face-down cards, your opponent chooses a card in their hand and puts it in Memory face-down, and draws a card.
Look at up to the top 4 cards of your deck, choose a <<Phantom Thief>> character among them, add it to your hand, and put the rest of the cards to the Waiting Room.
Choose one of your opponents Front Row Cost 0 or lower characters, and you may return it to their hand.

We've got several good cards here. The event is quite interesting, being a good 1/0 check 4 event with a few extra abilities. It's good for the check 4, but activating the 0/0's effect is an added bonus (even if it ultimately does allow your opponent to filter a card). It's still not an ideal filter for the opponent, as the card stays in Memory for a bit, so they probably won't use it to filter Climaxes and such, so it doesn't really matter too much that they get a filter.

The global 500 is good for being a global 500, but can also get a card under certain circumstances. Really good, but not much else to say. The search is convoluted, but global power is good, so we play it for the global effect anyways.

Lastly, we have a way to salvage our event. Important, considering how much it does. Not much else to say here either, its just a good filter and consistency booster.
