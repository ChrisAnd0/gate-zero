Luck & Logic - Card of the Day, November 29th 2016

Bullet Exercises, Mana
World: Tetraheaven
[A] COVENANT - <Mana> or 「Artemis」 If the deployed Tranceunion is 「Mana」, if you have 3 or more 「Mana」in your Level Zone, draw a card.

Moonlight of Mystery, Artemis
World: Tetraheaven
[A] COVENANT- 「Mana」 - If the deployed Tranceunion is 「Mana」, if you have 3 or more cards in your Level Zone, look at the top 3 cards of your deck, choose one of them, put it face-up into your Level Zone, put the rest back on top of your deck in any order, choose a face-up card in your Level Zone, and Drop it.

MANA SUPPORT.

So, if people haven't realized, I really like Mana. New Mana support is really exciting for me as a result. Anways, so with this card, Mana gets a much-needed universal Covenant, similar to the variousc cards in Aid & Arms. The first card allows you to draw a card, which is great, because who doesn't love free cards? Combined with Sky and Sea and Mana or Don't Give Up! Mana, Mana decks draw even more cards than ever. While I don't think it replaces either of the two prior Mana covenants, its certainly a strong additional option, and gives a strong Covenant before Level 4.

Secondly, we have a Artemis Covenant. This card allows us to not only replace a card out of our Level Zone (more easilly getting Manas in Level for the Mana Covenant and Trance Rare), but also lets us control the top 2 cards of the deck. And since Covenant abilities are resolved in any order, we can use to to control the card we draw with today's Mana. Good effects all around.

Basically, I really like Mana. She's really cute. And got me to second place at Nationals, so she's great. More support is amazing. I like drawing cards.
