We Have a Lot of Treasures
Trigger: Soul, Soul
[C] RECOLLECTION If this card is in your Memory, all of your characters with "Treasure" in the name get Power +4000.

So this is certainly one of the most complex effects in all of Weiβ Schwarz, and it breaks a few rules and introduces some new card types to get there.

Rule broken #1: 1/0s don't have Soul Triggers.
So 1/0s don't usually have Soul Triggers. And honestly, this kinda makes these cards a bit nuts. We can now play a deck with around 34 Soul Triggers that isn't expensive (and full of underpowered 1/1s) since, well, our costless 1/0s have Soul Triggers, meaning we can have good damage output even when we don't play a Climax. This isn't even counting the fact that the Cocoa and Rize give soul if you mill Soul Triggers. Easy, easy soul.

Rule broken #2: A climax that doesn't do anyhting in the Climax Zone.
So if your Climax doesn't do anything in the zone its intended for, it better do something good. I'd call giving everything 4k pretty good. 1/0 9k L1s is really good. 1/0 13ks is even better, except then you have a 6 Climax deck, so this bit might be too risky.

So, what do we get for literally building our deck around this combo? I think the most notable and overlooked thing is that the Level 1s all have Soul Triggers, meaning we can build a deck with 34-Soul Triggers without stock issues. Once upon a time, I played a deck of 42 vanillas, because it had 1/1 6k vanillas with Soul Triggers. This deck is remarkably good for what it does. Having a deck this trigger-heavy is crazy. Doing it for free? Where has this been all game?

Not to mention that it also still gets power and (some) cards, and extra soul. This deck is going to be crazy, just wait.
